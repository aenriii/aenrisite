## what is "radqueer"?

a radical queer (or "radqueer" for short) is someone who follows a social ideology
aligned along the premise of "automatically accept all good-faith identities."
starting at face value, this doesn't sound like something that should cause much
controversy, right?

### what's a good-faith identity?

[good faith](https://www.law.cornell.edu/wex/good_faith) is an idea not coined by,
but used within the united states legal system to mean, broadly, "honest dealing"
of some kind. for our use-case, this definition is more refined to "honest belief,"
thus defining a good faith identity as something that someone truly believes they are,
from relatively normalized terms such as transgender and lesbian to more niche terms
like therian and otherkin, and even often to typically outcasted identities such as
paraphilias and trans-ids
